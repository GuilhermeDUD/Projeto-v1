Ferrari Roma

2024

The Roma is one of the most exciting grand tourers today: precise proportions that adapt easily to urban traffic, a ride that can be sporty or comfortable, a luxurious interior and an engine that distributes power and torque.

While fully capable of delivering on-track performance worthy of the Ferrari badge – under the hood is a 611-horsepower 3.9 twin-turbo V8 mated to one of the smoothest and quickest dual-clutch transmissions available – the Roma Spider was designed for sweet life of the most beautiful places in the world.

The main difference is the folding fabric roof, as everything else is inherited from the coupe version. According to Ferrari, the roof opening and closing mechanisms have been improved, meaning that opening or closing the roof is a process that takes just 13 seconds – and can be carried out at up to 60 km/h.
